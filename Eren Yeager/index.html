About Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager is the protagonist of the anime and manga series "Attack on Titan." His journey from a vengeful child to a powerful warrior with the fate of humanity on his shoulders is one of the most captivating stories in anime.

Eren is known for his determination, intense emotions, and a desire to bring freedom to humanity. As the series progresses, Eren's character becomes increasingly complex, reflecting the harsh realities of the world he lives in.

Powers & Abilities

Titan Shifter

Eren possesses the power to transform into a Titan, a giant humanoid creature with immense strength and regenerative abilities. This power makes him one of humanity's greatest assets against the Titans.

Founding Titan

As the holder of the Founding Titan, Eren has the ability to control other Titans and manipulate the memories of Eldians. This power is key to understanding the mysteries of the world.

Attack Titan

The Attack Titan is known for its fighting prowess and its ability to inherit the will of its predecessors. Eren uses this power to push forward, even against overwhelming odds.

Character Development

Eren's character development is one of the most profound aspects of "Attack on Titan." From a young boy filled with hatred for Titans to a leader willing to sacrifice everything for freedom, Eren's journey is marked by significant events that shape his personality and actions.

  • Childhood: Eren's experiences during the fall of Shiganshina define his early motivations. His desire for revenge against the Titans drives him to join the military.
  • Survey Corps: As a member of the Survey Corps, Eren discovers the harsh realities of the world beyond the walls and begins to question his own beliefs and the intentions of those around him.
  • The Rumbling: Eren's decision to initiate the Rumbling is a pivotal moment, showcasing his willingness to go to extreme lengths to achieve his goals, even if it means becoming the very thing he once sought to destroy.

Major Battles

Battle of Trost District

Eren's first major battle as a Titan Shifter, where he helps humanity secure its first victory against the Titans.

Clash of the Titans

The fight against the Armored Titan and the Colossal Titan reveals the true identity of Eren's former comrades, leading to a dramatic confrontation.

The Raid on Liberio

Eren's attack on the Marleyan city of Liberio marks the beginning of his aggressive campaign against the world, showcasing his transformation into a feared leader.

Timeline

  • Year 835 - Birth of Eren Yeager

    Eren is born in Shiganshina District to Grisha and Carla Yeager.

  • Year 845 - Fall of Shiganshina

    Eren witnesses the destruction of his hometown by the Colossal and Armored Titans, sparking his hatred for Titans and desire for revenge.

  • Year 850 - Graduation and Joining the Survey Corps

    Eren graduates from the 104th Training Corps and joins the Survey Corps, determined to fight Titans and discover the truth about the world.

  • Year 851 - Discovery of the Truth

    Eren learns the dark secrets behind the Titans and the world beyond the walls, changing his perception of humanity's fight for survival.

  • Year 854 - The Rumbling

    Eren initiates the Rumbling, unleashing the power of the Founding Titan and setting the stage for a catastrophic global conflict.
