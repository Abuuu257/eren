Major Battles

Battle of Trost

One of Eren’s earliest and most defining battles, the Battle of Trost, was the first time he transformed into a Titan. This battle marked the beginning of his struggle to harness his newfound power and protect humanity.

Battle with the Female Titan

This battle against Annie Leonhart, the Female Titan, tested Eren’s resolve and combat skills. It was a crucial moment in understanding the complexity of the Titans and the conspiracies within the walls.

Battle of Shiganshina

The Battle of Shiganshina was a turning point in the war against the Titans. Eren played a key role in reclaiming his hometown and uncovering the secrets hidden in the Yeager family basement.

Battle of Liberio

The Battle of Liberio saw Eren taking the fight to Marley, showcasing his new powers and his willingness to take extreme measures to protect his people, even at great personal cost.

The Rumbling

The final and most devastating battle led by Eren, the Rumbling was a cataclysmic event aimed at annihilating the world outside the walls. It was the culmination of Eren’s beliefs and actions, leading to a global conflict.
