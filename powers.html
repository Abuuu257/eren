Powers & Abilities

Founding Titan

The Founding Titan is one of the Nine Titans, and grants its user the ability to control Titans and alter the memories of Eldians. Eren gained this ability after eating his father, Grisha Yeager, who had inherited it from the Reiss family.

Attack Titan

The Attack Titan allows Eren to access future memories of its inheritors, which motivates his actions throughout the series. It also grants him immense strength, speed, and endurance.

War Hammer Titan

The War Hammer Titan grants Eren the ability to create structures and weapons out of hardened Titan flesh, a unique ability among the Nine Titans. Eren gained this power after defeating Lara Tybur.

Additional Abilities

  • Regeneration: Eren possesses the ability to rapidly heal from injuries, including regenerating entire limbs.
  • Enhanced Strength: His Titan form exhibits incredible physical power, capable of decimating other Titans and fortified structures.
  • Strategic Intelligence: Over time, Eren develops a keen strategic mind, using his powers and knowledge to outmaneuver his enemies.
  • Coordination: Eren can coordinate his Titan powers with his allies, making him a formidable leader in battle.
