Timeline

Early Life

Eren Yeager was born in Shiganshina District. He grew up with his adoptive sister Mikasa Ackerman and his best friend Armin Arlert, dreaming of exploring the world beyond the walls.

Battle of Trost

During the Battle of Trost, Eren discovers his ability to transform into a Titan and fights to defend the city from the invading Titans.

Discovery of the Female Titan

Eren and his comrades face off against the Female Titan, who is later revealed to be Annie Leonhart, a former trainee soldier.

Battle of Shiganshina

Eren plays a critical role in reclaiming his hometown, Shiganshina, from the Titans and uncovers key information about his father's past.

The Rumbling

Eren initiates the Rumbling, a catastrophic event to destroy the world outside the walls and protect his homeland. This leads to a global conflict and significant changes in the world order.
